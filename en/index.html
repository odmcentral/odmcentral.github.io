About ODM

The Optimized Decision Making (ODM) is a company founded in 2017 by former researchers from the Laboratory of Optimization of University of São Paulo - Brazil.

The ODM team consists of masters and doctors with a broad experience in solving industrial problems at all levels: operational, tactical and strategic. ODM's expertise is to identify, model and propose solutions to any problems in applied mathematics. Such solutions are delivered in the form of reports, consultancies and computer systems customized to your demand.



Why choose ODM?


The team

Our professionals are postgraduates in the fields of applied mathematics, industrial engineering and computer science at universities of excellence. The high degree of qualification of these professionals reflects in the quality of the consultancies and services that we provide.

The best decision making

Search the best solution for a given problem is a science that brings together knowledge of artificial intelligence, operations research and optimization. ODM professionals have competence in these areas proven by their academic training and international projects they developed.

Usability

The products developed by ODM have a user-friendly interface and are easy to use by users of varied profiles. All products are validated in the company and so are customized for your needs, impacting on the results and directly on your profit.

Confidentiality and security

The information is strategic for your business. Therefore, the entire ODM team is committed to the confidentiality of the information presented during the consultancies and the products we develop have a high degree of reliability in information security.

Development time

The ODM professionals have extensive experience in mathematical modeling and in the development of computer systems for the resolution of industrial problems. In addition, the organizational structure of ODM and our agile methodology for project management and planning reflect in a low development time.

Support and service

The support for ODM products is offered by its respective developers, with good communication and service practices. Our support is done during business hours by e-mail or telephone, with the possibility of being on-site or remote. This ensures a responsive and effective service for your company.


Development methodology


Training

Keep your team always updated. We have theoretical and practical training, on-site and on distance for your team always get ahead. The training covers the theoretical part of operations research and optimization, such as mathematical modeling, linear, integer and non-linear programming. We also offer practical training in a variety of programming languages and optimization software.


Contact us and check our availability.


Customized trainning

The preparation of each training is performed with a careful look at your team needs. Thus, the training becomes more effective, making the team able to use and apply the topics presented with more confidence and precision.

On site or live broadcast

Our training is offered on site or distance. The on site mode, one or more ODM employees will go to your company to provide the training. In the distance option, our employees live broadcast the course for a certain group of people.

Post-training support

We offer post-training support service. With it, even after the training, questions about the topics covered can be asked for our team.

The problems we address

Supply chain

Our team can model, optimize and propose computational tools to solve the Supply Chain problem of your company.

Packing problems

We have robust computational tools to reduce costs and waste with one-dimensional, two-dimensional cutting problems such as rectangles and complex items such as gears. In addition, we can reduce the waste of space on packaging problems for three-dimensional items.

Tansportation and logistcs

Want to find the best way to deliver or collect your products? Our algorithms find the minimum cost route, reducing the waste of time and money.

Production sequencing and scheduling

Have you ever thought about organizing your production or scheduling the best sequence to produce your products? We have the most economical solution. Production scheduling and sequencing is a specialty of ODM.
